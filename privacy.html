Privacy Policy

Dashflix respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use this site and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Dashflix does not require you to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or payment details.

Local storage: Your watchlist is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key df_wl. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us. Similarly, your theme preference is stored locally under df_theme.

Usage data: Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server log information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time of access. This data is used only for security and operational purposes and is not linked to any personal identity.

Cookies

Dashflix itself does not set first-party cookies. However, third-party services embedded on this site — including video players and advertising networks — may set their own cookies. Please refer to their individual privacy policies for details.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • The Movie Database (TMDb) — provides metadata (titles, posters, ratings). Subject to TMDb's Privacy Policy.
  • Third-party video embed providers — stream video content when you click Watch. Each provider operates independently and has its own privacy and cookie policy. Dashflix has no control over these services.
  • Google AdSense — displays advertisements. Google may collect data to serve personalised ads. See Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses cookies in advertising.
  • Font Awesome CDN — serves icon fonts. Subject to Font Awesome's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Dashflix is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Data Security

Since Dashflix does not collect or store personal data on its servers, there is no centralised database of user information to protect. Your watchlist data resides exclusively on your own device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of Dashflix after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please email us at support@dashflix.top.
