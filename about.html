About Dashflix

A free, no-signup streaming portal that aggregates movies and TV shows from across the web — built for people who just want to watch.

What is Dashflix?

Dashflix is a free streaming aggregator. We don't produce, store, or host any video content. Instead, we surface publicly available streams from third-party video providers and present them through a clean, unified interface — so you spend less time searching and more time watching.

All movie and TV show metadata — titles, posters, ratings, descriptions, cast — is sourced from The Movie Database (TMDb), one of the world's largest open movie databases.

Features

  • Completely free — no subscription, no credit card, ever.
  • No account required — just open the site and start watching immediately.
  • Massive library — thousands of movies and TV shows across every genre and era.
  • Multiple servers — switch streams instantly if one isn't working.
  • Watchlist — save titles locally in your browser to watch later.
  • Android app — download the native app for a better mobile experience.
  • Dark & light theme — switch modes to suit your environment.
  • Smart filters — filter by genre, year, and sort order on browse pages.

How It Works

When you click Watch on a title, Dashflix loads a stream from one of several third-party embed providers. These providers are independent services — Dashflix has no control over their content availability, regional restrictions, or uptime. If one stream doesn't work, try switching to a different server on the player page.

Your watchlist is stored entirely in your browser's localStorage — nothing is sent to any server. Clearing your browser data will clear your watchlist.

Technology

Dashflix is a fully client-side web application built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no backend, no build tools. Content metadata is fetched directly from the TMDb API. Video playback is handled by third-party iframe embeds.
